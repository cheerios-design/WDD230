Site Plan

Site Name

Ankara Chamber of Commerce

Site Purpose

The Ankara Chamber of Commerce website serves as a hub for local businesses and community members, providing:

  1. Comprehensive information about the business community in Ankara.
  2. Resources for businesses to join the chamber and enhance their presence in the community.

Scenarios

  • Businesses: "How can I join the chamber and access resources to grow my business in Ankara?"
  • Visitors: "What are the key attractions and events happening in Ankara right now?"
  • New Residents: "Which local businesses are members of the chamber, and what services do they offer?"
